Overview

A strategic relaunch of an
enterprise L&D function.

Co-led the strategic relaunch and transformation of an enterprise Learning & Development function following organizational disruption. Designed and implemented a scalable governance model, stakeholder engagement framework, internal capability-building programs, and data-driven evaluation systems to reposition L&D as a strategic performance partner. The initiative increased internal demand, strengthened cross-functional alignment, and expanded enterprise capability without increasing headcount.
